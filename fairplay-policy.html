Fairplay Policy

Simply put, Fairplay policy is ensuring all players have the same set of rules and no unfair advantage to any player in any means. We ensure the same by following below:
  1. One Person - One Mobile Device - One Mobile number: Cases where Users utilising multiple mobile numbers for undue winning edge, if found, are treated as violation to our fairplay policy and necessary action - as much as permanent ban - may be taken for the given user.
  2. Utilising hacks or bots or cheating would be a violation and is strictly prohibited. Serious action - as much as permanent ban - may be taken for the given user.
  3. Users to ensure authentic and correct KYC and other documents and usage of Fraudulent ID proofs and KYC proofs is strictly prohibited, with legal consequences.
  4. Users found using vulgar and abusive language, racist and abusive remarks is strictly prohibited and necessary action is taken against them.
  5. Wherever the users are found to exploit or violate the fair-play rules, MakeMyWin reserves the right to impose ban, seize user wallets and impose penalties as high as 10x the amount won by the user and freezes the account of the user anywhere from 30 days to 240 days and may be taken on case-to-case basis.
  6. Genuine users can report all issues pertaining to the fairplay violations at support@makemywin.com and necessary action would be taken. We take user experience and trust as non-negotiable and treated with utmost respect.
  7. This fairplay policy would be updated from time to time and users are requested to check this section accordingly. Last update on this policy - 11th Feb 2022
